All Courses

All three streams—Science, Arts, and Commerce—are available for students to choose from, offering a wide range of academic opportunities that cater to diverse interests and career paths. Whether one is inclined towards the logical and analytical aspects of Science, the creative and expressive nature of Arts, or the practical and business-oriented field of Commerce, all options are accessible to help shape a successful future.

Labs

We have fully equipped laboratories for Chemistry, Physics, Biology, and Computer Science, each designed to provide students with hands-on experience and a deeper understanding of their respective subjects. These labs are equipped with state-of-the-art tools and resources, ensuring an enriching learning environment that fosters experimentation, discovery, and innovation in the fields of science and technology.

It visits

In addition to our academic labs, we also offer specialized vocational labs designed to provide practical training and skill development in various trades and professions. To further enhance students' real-world learning experience, we organize industrial visits, allowing them to observe and engage with professionals in actual work environments. These opportunities bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, preparing students for a successful career in their chosen fields.
